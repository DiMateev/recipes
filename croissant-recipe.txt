250gr butter
200gr wheat
120ml water
3gr yeast
3gr sugar

Mix all but butter and make a dough. Cut butter thin and wrap it with the dough. Chill in fridge.
Fold the dough 3 more times. Chill in fridge. Repeat 3 times last two steps. Then cut triangles from the dough
and roll them up in form of croissants. Preheat oven to 180C and bake for 24 minutes or until goldish.
Enjoy!
